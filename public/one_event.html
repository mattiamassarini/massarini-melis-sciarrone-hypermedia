Description:
Queen’s famous album celebrates his 54th birthday, and many songs contained in the album are real rock anthem, even today, and are popular among youngsters, also for the released movie“Bohemian Rhapsody”. The famous orchestra Rossini from Italy is going to perform all the greatest singles from the band along with Brian May and Roger Taylor, two Queens’ members. For this special show, Freddie Mercury’s magical voice and lyrics will be taken over by Andrea Bocelli, so this is a must-be show!
